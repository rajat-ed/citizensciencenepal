About Us

Citizen Science Nepal is a pioneering platform dedicated to fostering scientific research and data collection through community participation. We believe that everyone has a role to play in understanding and preserving our natural world. Our mission is to connect citizens with scientific projects, enabling them to contribute valuable data, learn new skills, and make a tangible impact on research and conservation efforts across Nepal.
